# Operations Guide

## Routines

Consistency beats intensity. These routines keep the business running without heroics.

### Daily

- Check and respond to client messages (target: under 4 hours)
- Review today's task list — reschedule what won't fit
- Log any decisions made in `planning/decisions.md`

### Weekly

- Review pipeline: what's active, what's stalled, what needs follow-up
- Update `planning/weekly-plan.md` with priorities for next week
- Send any pending client updates or status reports
- Review financial reports: invoices sent, payments received, outstanding

### Monthly

- Review goals vs. actual figures — what hit, what missed, why
- Update `planning/quarterly-goals.md` with progress
- Client relationship check: anyone going quiet? Proactive outreach.
- Review and update SOPs in `resources/` — does anything need revision?

### Quarterly

- Strategic review: is the business moving toward the annual goals?
- Client portfolio review: profitability, satisfaction, fit
- Update pricing based on costs, market, and value delivered
- Clean up files, archive completed projects, update templates

## Decision Framework

When facing a business decision:

1. **What problem does this solve?** If you can't name it, don't do it.
2. **What's the cost of doing nothing?** Sometimes waiting is the right move.
3. **Is this reversible?** Reversible decisions go fast. Irreversible ones get research.
4. **Who does this affect?** Clients, team, finances — check all three.

Log every significant decision in `planning/decisions.md` with: date, context, decision, reasoning.

## Client Response Templates

Keep templates in `resources/` for:

- New inquiry response
- Project status update
- Scope change acknowledgment
- Invoice follow-up
- Project completion and next steps

Customize every template before sending. Templates save structure, not personality.
